Claire King is a filmmaker and installation artist living and working in Minneapolis, Minnesota. She is interested in the spaces between people: where interaction becomes autonomous, perception is corporeal, and expression has consequence. Working in film, video installation, and collaborative, body-based practice, King's work seeks to realize the radical imagination. Her current project, The Cloud Suit, is a multi media experiment in controlling perception; to allow a body to be seen as a phenomenon; indistinct, ephemeral, and subject to change.

Claire King is also a curator, restaurant owner, and accountant.
